Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your visit

How do I book an appointment?

You can book through WhatsApp, call us directly, or use our online booking system. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for the best availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please cancel at least 12 hours before your appointment. Late cancellations may incur a fee. No-shows will be charged the full service amount.

Do you offer walk-in services?

Yes, we accept walk-ins based on availability. However, we strongly recommend booking an appointment to ensure you get served at your preferred time.

What products do you use?

We use premium, professional-grade products from top brands. All products are available for purchase in our retail section.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! We offer physical and digital gift cards in any amount. Perfect for gifts or treating yourself to premium grooming.

How long does a typical service take?

Classic cuts take 30-45 minutes, premium services 45-60 minutes, and VIP experiences 60-90 minutes. Your stylist will give you an exact time estimate.
